* Org mode Goban

A Goban diagramming tool for Emacs Org-mode.

Early days, but lets you do stuff like this :

#+begin_src goban :file amazing.svg
$$B
$$ . . . . . . . . |
$$ . . . . . . O . |
$$ . . . . . . 8 6 |
$$ . . . . 4 O 2 5 |
$$ . . X 10 9 3 1 7 |
$$ . . . . . . . . |
$$ ----------------+
#+end_src
